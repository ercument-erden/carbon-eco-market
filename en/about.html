Our Journey

2024 Q4 - Foundation

CarbonEcoMarket idea was born. World-class advisory board formed: C-level executives from ACWA Power, Borusan EnBW and ÇEKÜL.

2025 Q1 - LOI Collection

Collected 12 Letters of Intent from chemical, cement and automotive sectors. Total 50K ton/year potential pipeline.

2025 Q2 - MVP Development

CBAM-compliant platform MVP is being completed. Blockchain registry working on testnet. Seed round initiated.

2025 Q4 - Pilot Launch

Platform goes live with first 10-20 pilot customers. Real carbon emission data collection begins.

2026 Q1 - CBAM Mandatory

EU CBAM obligation begins. 15,000+ Turkish exporters must report. Major growth opportunity.

2027-2030 - Scaling

Expansion to MENA region. Farmer and individual user modules go live. 6-sided ecosystem operating at full capacity.
