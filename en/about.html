Our Journey

2024 Q4 - Foundation

CarbonEcoMarket idea was born. World-class advisory board established: C-level executives from ACWA Power, Borusan EnBW and ÇEKÜL.

2025 Q1 - LOI Collection

12 Letters of Intent collected from chemical, cement and automotive sectors. Total 50K tons/year potential pipeline.

2025 Q2 - MVP Development

CBAM-compliant platform MVP being completed. Blockchain registry running on testnet. Seed round initiated.

2026 Q1 - CBAM Mandatory

EU CBAM obligation begins. 15,000+ Turkish exporters must report. Major growth opportunity.

2027-2030 - Scaling

MENA region expansion. Launch of farmer and individual user modules. 6-sided ecosystem at full capacity.
